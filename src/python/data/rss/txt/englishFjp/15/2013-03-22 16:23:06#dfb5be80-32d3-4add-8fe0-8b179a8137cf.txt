Khalid Hanafi: Freedom and Justice Party Election Platform Near Completion
On the sidelines of an internal elections committee meeting, FJP Secretary Hanafi reveals some details of the party’s election platform – almost complete and ready for release.
Dr. Khalid Hanafi, Secretary of the Freedom and Justice Party (FJP) in Cairo, confirmed that the party will raise the proportion of youth participation in FJP candidate lists (for forthcoming legislative elections) to 30%.
"The majority of FJP candidate lists will also include a female nominee, so as to ensure fair representation of youth and women in the upcoming House of Representatives.
On the sidelines of a meeting of the FJP’s Central Election Committee, Dr. Hanafi added that the party's platform for the approaching parliamentary polls is nearing completion, and will offer sensible solutions to practical problems that touch the lives of citizens.
