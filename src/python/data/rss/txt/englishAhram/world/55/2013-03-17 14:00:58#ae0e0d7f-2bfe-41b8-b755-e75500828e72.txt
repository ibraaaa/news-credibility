EU embassy in Cairo denies Catherine Ashton's visit to Egypt
The EU Embassy in Cairo quashes media rumours on Catherine Ashton's visit to Egypt on Sunday
The EU Embassy in Egypt denies media reports that the EU High Representative for Foreign Affairs and Security Catherine Ashton will pay a visit to Egypt on Sunday.
In a brief statement issued on Sunday morning, the EU embassy in Cairo said that the news released by some media and news sites that Ashton will arrive in Cairo on Sunday "are false and untrue."
