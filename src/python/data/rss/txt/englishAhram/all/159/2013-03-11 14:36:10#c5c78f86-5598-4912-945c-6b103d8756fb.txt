VIDEO: Theater project reacts to current events in Ard El-Lewa
'The Living Newspaper' in Artellewa offers workshops and bi-weekly performances a space for artistic reaction to current events and social issues in Egypt
In the heart of Ard El-Lewa neighbourhood on the outskirts of Giza, lies an art gallery and centre that aims to serve the community: Artellewa.
The Living Newspaper
is one of the space's newer projects and aims to create a space for people to artistically react to the fast-paced news and events currently taking place in Egypt through theatre, installations and puppetry.
