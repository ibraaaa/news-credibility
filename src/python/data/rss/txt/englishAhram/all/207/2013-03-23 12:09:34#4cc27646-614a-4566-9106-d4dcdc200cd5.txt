Ahly to face England's Fulham in friendly
The African champions are set to face English Premier League side Fulham in a friendly game
 
Ahly have accepted an offer to play a friendly game against English Premier League side Fulham, the Cairo club said on Saturday.
"Ahly will play a friendly game against Fulham of England during the coming period," read a statement on the Red's official website.
"The match's date will be determined according to team's fixtures list and commitments in the African Champions League and the Egyptian Premier League," the statement added.
Ahly are looking to win an eighth consecutive league title. They are also chasing a second successive Champions League accolade, having won a record-extending seventh title last year.
