Inter defender Ranocchia out of Milan derby
Inter Milan defender Andrea Ranocchia will miss the derby with AC Milan on Sunday (1945) after sustaining a knee injury during Thursday's 3-0 Europa League win over CFR Cluj
The 25-year-old suffered ligament damage to his right knee, ANSA news service reported
.
Inter are fifth in Serie A on 43 points, only a point and two places behind a resurgent Milan.
