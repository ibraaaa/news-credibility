Blasts hit Shi'ite districts in Baghdad, killing 9 - police
At least 9 people were killed and 30 injured following several bombs at an Iraqi Shiite neighborhood
A series of bombs exploded in mainly Shi'ite neighbourhoods across the Iraqi capital Baghdad on Sunday, killing at least nine people and wounding more than 30, police said.
The bombs detonated in the Ameen, Sadr City, Habibiya and Qahira districts of the capital.
