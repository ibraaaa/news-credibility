VIDEO: Alexandrian wind plays music in Sawteyat
Displayed in front of the Bibliotheca Alexandrina on 26 and 28 February, 'Sawteyat' project uses handmade instruments with specific notes designed to be played by the wind
The installation, displayed in front of the Bibliotheca Alexandrina, on 26 and 28 February during the afternoons, challenges the social stereotype of what music is while pesenting a different type of art in public space in Egypt's second largest city.
