Ennahda offices attacked, protests in several Tunisia towns
Offices of the ruling party attacked after opposition leader shot dead
Ennahda offices attacked, protests erupted  in several Tunisia towns in the wake of the killing of leftist politician Shokri Belaid on Wednesday.
Belaid was shot dead outside his home on Wednesday, in a killing that Prime Minister Hamadi Jebali condemned as a political assasination and a strike against the "Arab Spring" revolutions.
