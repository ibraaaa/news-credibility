Ghanaian youngster Antoi scores twice as Ismaily maintain perfect start
Ismaily collect maximum nine points from three Egyptian Premier League games after beating Dakhleya on Sunday
Ghanaian striker John Antoi scored twice to help Ismaili maintain their perfect Egyptian Premier League start with a 2-1 victory over Dakhleya on Sunday.
Antoi, 20, opened the score for Ismaily in the 28th minute but Dakhleya’s Nigerian defender Jude Ebitogwa netted the equalizer in the second half.
The Costal side kept on pressing after the break and their pressure paid dividends when Antoi scored the winner with 14 minutes remaining.
Ismaily are on top of Group B with nine points, on level with Cairo giants Zamalek.
