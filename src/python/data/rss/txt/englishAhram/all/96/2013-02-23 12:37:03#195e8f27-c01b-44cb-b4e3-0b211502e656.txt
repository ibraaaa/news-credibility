Egypt's Abou Moslem scores first goal with Lierse
Abou Moslem opens his account with Belgian side Lierse during their 2-0 win over Beerschot on Saturday
Egyptian defender Ahmed Abou Moslem scored his first goal with struggling Belgian side Lierse in 45th minute of the team's 2-0 win over Beerschot in the Belgian league on Saturday
Abou Moslem, who joined Lierse last October from Egyptian Premier League outfit Smouha, managed to get on the scoresheet in his fifth appearance with the team.
The 31-year-old is among seven Egyptian footballers playing at Lierse, who are owned by Egyptian businessman Maged Sami and managed by Egyptian coach Hany Ramzy. 
 
They lie 14th in the Belgian league table with 23 points.
