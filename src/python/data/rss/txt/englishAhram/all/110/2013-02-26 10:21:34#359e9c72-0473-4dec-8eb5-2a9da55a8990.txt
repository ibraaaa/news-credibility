Maqassa claim first league win in last game for outgoing manager
Maqassa beat Wadi Degla for their first Egyptian Premier League win this season
Maqassa ended their winless streak in their last match under outgoing manager Mohamed Abdel-Galil after claiming a 1-0 victory over fellow struggling side Wadi Degla on Tuesday.
Mohamed Farouk scored what proved to be the winner in the 49
th
minute as Maqassa recovered from a poor start that saw them lose two and draw once in their first three matches.
They now lie third from bottom in Group A with four points from four games.
Abdel-Galil will now make way for Egypt's icon Hossam Hassan, who will take over at the club until the end of the season.
