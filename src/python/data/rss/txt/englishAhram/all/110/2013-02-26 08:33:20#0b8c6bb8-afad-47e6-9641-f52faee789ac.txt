Iran, world powers adjourn talks, to meet Wednesday
Iran and world powers concluded a day of discussions in Kazakhstan over the Islamic republic's disputed nuclear programme and will meet again on Wednesday, officials from both sides said.
"We had a useful meeting today. Discussions took place this evening, (and) we are meeting again tomorrow," said a Western official. An Iranian source close to the talks said the negotiations would continue Wednesday.
