Egypt's Wama releases patriotic song
Wama releases patriotic song for Egypt prior to launch of new album in April
After a two-year-break, Egyptian music group Wama returns with a patriotic song entitled
(Let's Do Something For Her), prior to the launch of its new album in April.
According to Al-Ahram Arabic website, Wama's new song calls on Egyptians to work hard and help return Egypt to its proper place in the world.
Tamer Hussein wrote the song and the music was composed by Ahmed Fahmy.
Wama's next album is scheduled to be released in April in celebration of the band's tenth anniversary.
