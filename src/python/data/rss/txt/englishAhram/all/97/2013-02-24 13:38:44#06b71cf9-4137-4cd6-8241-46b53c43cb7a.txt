Kaka praises Real Madrid players for Deportivo win
Real Madrid midfielder is happy for helping his team to beat Deportivo in the Spanish La Liga
Real Madrid attacking midfielder Kaka has said he and his teammates did their best throughout the second half of their 2-1 win over Deportivo La Coruna in the Spanish La Liga on Saturday.
“The team is playing well and things are getting better for me too," Kaka was quoted as saying on the club's official website.
The Meringues came from behind and managed to beat Deportivo 2-1 in a match in which Kaka stole the show due to his superb performance and scoring the winning goal.
"In football everything changes quickly and I am happy to help the team win," Kaka said.
“In the first half we were a little anxious and we made a few mistakes.
"In the second half we improved and we won three points that are important for us and for what is to come over the next weeks.”
Real Madrid are third with 52 points after also having to come from behind to beat bottom side Deportivo 2-1 with late goals from Kaka and Gonzalo Higuain.
