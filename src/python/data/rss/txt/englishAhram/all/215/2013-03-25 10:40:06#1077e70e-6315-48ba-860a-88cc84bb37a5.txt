FIFA U-20 World Cup Draw List
Draw list conducted Monday for the FIFA Under-20 World Cup, being played June 21-July 13 in Turkey
Spain, African team, United States, France
South Korea, African team, Cuba, Portugal
Colombia, Australia, El Salvador, Turkey
Mexico, African team, Paraguay, Greece
African champion, Iraq, Chile, England
New Zealand or Fiji, Uzbekistan, Uruguay, Croatia
African teams Egypt, Ghana, Mali, Nigeria will be allocated to groups after the Under-20 African Championships is completed on March 30.
