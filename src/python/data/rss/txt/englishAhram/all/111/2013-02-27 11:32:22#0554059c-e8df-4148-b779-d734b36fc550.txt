Nuclear talks between world powers, Iran conclude: Officials
Iran and world powers conclude the two-day nuclear talks in Kazakhstan; further details still to be provided
Two days of talks between Iran and world powers aimed at breaking the decade-long deadlock over the Islamic republic's nuclear programme concluded on Wednesday in Kazakhstan, Western and Iranian officials said.
"Talks have concluded," said a Western official, adding that details of the meeting would be provided shortly. An Iranian official also confirmed that the talks had now concluded, without giving further details.
